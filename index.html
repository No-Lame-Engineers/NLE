NO_LAME_ENGINEERS

NLE – is an open organization of engineering-minded fellows, that is proud to welcome aboard any engineer that is not considered to be lame

It may cause anger and despair, but you should keep in mind that everything should be taken with a grain of salt. Everything, except this one

You are considered to be a lame engineer if you:

  • Are toxic
  • Think impossible exists
  • Underestimate simplicity
  • Have no aesthetic feeling
  • Think hierarchy is a mean to dominate others
  • Want to reach heights without conquering yourself
  • Are not on a pilgrimage of seeking your true self
  • Forget to put “;” (semicolon) at the end of JavaScript statements
  • Not open to exploring new ideas and welcome constructive criticism
