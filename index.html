NO LAME ENGINEERS

NLE – is a community of engineering-minded fellows, that is proud to welcome aboard any not lame engineer.
Our goals are to learn, share and... engineer

Following information has no intent to offend anyone, but rather raise awareness. Nevertheless, it may cause anger and despair, but you should keep in mind that everything should be taken with a grain of salt. Everything, except this one

You are considered to be a lame engineer if you:

  • Are toxic
  • Think impossible exists
  • Underestimate simplicity
  • Have no aesthetic feeling
  • Are not on a pilgrimage of seeking your true self
  • Want to reach heights without conquering yourself
  • Think of hierarchy as a means of dominating others
  • Forget to put “;” (semicolon) at the end of JavaScript statements
  • Not open to exploring new ideas and welcome constructive criticism
